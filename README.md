# 🎓 AttendEase – Smart Attendance Management System

A modern and interactive web-based attendance management system designed for efficient student tracking, reporting, and analytics.

---

## 🌐 Live Demo

👉 https://geethika1531.github.io/attendance-system/

---

## ✨ Features

### 👩‍🎓 Student Management

* Add, edit, and delete students
* Search and filter students easily
* Track individual attendance percentage

### 📅 Attendance System

* Mark attendance (Present / Absent / Late)
* Quick actions (Mark all present/absent)
* Subject-wise attendance tracking

### 📊 Dashboard

* Total students overview
* Daily attendance summary
* Average attendance analytics
* Low attendance alerts

### 📑 Records & Reports

* Filter by date and student
* Detailed attendance logs
* Individual performance reports
* Visual progress indicators

---
